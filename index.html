About Us
  • When it turns on and when it records?

    When you put Moment on the kitchen and turn it on, it starts detecting the motions. Only when it matches particular motions, it starts recording as cooking and sends you URL via SMS. Moment does not record unless it matches particular motions even Moment is on.

  • Where the data will be stored?

    The data are captured by Moment device and are uploaded and saved in a secured place. It will not be possible to publish or share in any format without your consent.

  • Is it possible to turn on and off the camera?

    Yes. But device is design to be turned on all the time.

  • The audio will be recorded?

    No. There is no microphone
